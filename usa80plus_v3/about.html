About the USA 80+ Hockey Hall of Fame

Celebrating the passion, dedication, and achievements of hockey players and builders aged 80 and over.

Founded to honor the enduring spirit of senior hockey players, the USA 80+ Hockey Hall of Fame celebrates individuals aged 80 and above who continue to inspire on and off the ice. These are not just athletes — they are pioneers, mentors, and lifelong stewards of the game.

Our Hall of Fame resides at the Blue Sport Stable in Superior, Colorado. This fitting location was chosen in part due to its ties to The Real Iron Men of Hockey — a group whose resilience, camaraderie, and love for the game sparked a movement that led to the creation of this Hall.

We invite you to nominate a hockey legend, attend an upcoming celebration, or simply explore the stories of our inspiring inductees. Join us in honoring those who show that hockey truly is a lifelong passion.
