USA 80+ Hockey Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony

October 4, 2025 — Blue Sport Stable

Afternoon "Legends" Game, Reception, and Induction Banquet

People play hockey into their 80's and beyond? There is actually a USA Hockey Hall of Fame for such achievers? And it’s located at our own Blue Sport Stable? And 35 individuals will be inducted into the first class of the 80+ HHOF this Fall? YUP!

Join us on October 4 for the Legends Game, followed by a reception sponsored by Long Hockey, and an evening Induction Banquet celebrating these incredible individuals.

Teaser Information

  • Bill Goodacre – Honored with a special memorial
  • Mark Sertich – Guinness World Record holder, oldest hockey player in the world
  • Charles Schulz – Founder of the Snoopy World Ice Hockey Tournament
  • Ginette Hatin – Only female still playing at 80+
  • Terry Harper and Bill Orban – Only past NHL players still playing at 80+
  • 7 members of the USA 80+ Hockey Team (from Colorado) competing in the USA vs. Canada series
  • Other inspiring players and “builders” of the 80+ hockey community
