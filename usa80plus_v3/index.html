About the USA 80+ Hockey Hall of Fame

The USA 80+ Hockey Hall of Fame celebrates the passion, dedication, and achievements of hockey players and builders aged 80 and over. Our mission is to honor those whose love for the game defies age and inspires generations. By recognizing these exceptional individuals, we hope to encourage future players to embrace the sport with the same enthusiasm and perseverance.

The Hall of Fame is located at the Blue Sport Stable and was inspired by the legacy of The Real Iron Men of Hockey. We are grateful for the support of the hockey community and invite everyone to join us in celebrating these remarkable athletes and contributors.
